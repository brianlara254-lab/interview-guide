## Key Improvements Made:

### 1. Stronger Opening
- **Before**: "I'm a GenAI / AI Engineer focused on..."
- **After**: "AI Platform Engineer building..." / "Principal AI Engineer | ..."
- **Why**: Removes filler words, establishes seniority immediately

### 2. Specific Technologies
- **Added**: LangGraph, CrewAI, Vector DBs, Docker/K8s, AWS/GCP
- **Why**: Recruiters search for these keywords; shows hands-on experience

### 3. Quantified Impact
- **Added**: "10K+ requests/day", "production systems"
- **Why**: Proof of scale differentiates from toy projects

### 4. Clear Value Proposition
- **Before**: Focus on what you do
- **After**: Focus on problems you solve + outcomes
- **Why**: Companies hire for outcomes, not skills

### 5. Industry Keywords (2024-2025)
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** - Anthropic's new standard, trending
- **Agentic Systems** - Current hot topic
- **RAG** - Still critical but now assumed baseline
- **Observability-first** - Shows production mindset
- **Event-driven** - Architecture pattern employers seek

### 6. Removed/Refined
- ❌ "Not demos" → ✅ "Production systems that handle real-world load"
- ❌ "the way backend platforms are designed" → ✅ Direct architecture terms
- ❌ Long sentences → ✅ Short, scannable lines
